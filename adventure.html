Explore exciting activities near our hotel

Rafting

Experience the thrill of white-water rafting just a stone's throw away from our hotel. Nestled amidst the breathtaking natural beauty of our surroundings, our location offers the perfect gateway to exhilarating rafting adventures. Whether you're a seasoned rafter or a first-timer, you'll be captivated by the rushing waters and stunning landscapes that await you. With experienced guides and top-notch equipment, you can embark on a memorable rafting journey that combines heart-pounding excitement with the tranquility of nature. Come and join us for an unforgettable rafting experience right here in the heart of our hotel's serene surroundings.

Mountain Biking

Our well-maintained mountain bikes and safety equipment ensure a safe and enjoyable adventure. You'll have the opportunity to discover hidden gems, encounter local wildlife, and witness breathtaking vistas along the way. As you navigate the diverse terrain, you'll find yourself immersed in the serenity of nature and the joy of outdoor exploration.So, gear up, hop on a mountain bike, and embark on an adventure that combines fitness, fun, and natural beauty.

Hot Air Balooning

Our hot air balloon experiences offer a unique perspective, allowing you to see the beauty of our region from a bird's-eye view. With the earth below and the heavens above, you'll feel a sense of tranquility and awe as you soar with the gentle breeze. Our experienced pilots ensure a safe and memorable journey, pointing out landmarks and sharing fascinating insights about the area's geography and history. Whether you're celebrating a special occasion or simply seeking a serene escape, a hot air balloon ride offers an unparalleled sense of freedom and wonder. Capture stunning photographs, savor the peace and quiet, and create lasting memories as you drift gracefully through the skies. It's a truly magical experience that will leave you with a sense of awe and appreciation for the natural beauty that surrounds our hotel.

OUTDOOR MOVIE NIGHT

Venue

Strategically situated on the shore, our Beachside Movie Night Venue provides unimpeded views of the horizon and the sound of lapping waves as your background music. While stargazing, visitors may bury their toes in the warm, supple sand.

We provide a variety of sitting alternatives for your convenience, from luxurious beach chairs to soft blankets and cushions. Stretch down and unwind or cuddle up next to loved ones to create the ideal setting for a family reunion or a romantic evening.

Movie Selection

Welcome to our Movie Library, a treasure trove of cinematic delights designed to cater to every taste and preference. Nestled within the heart of our hotel, this curated collection of films invites you to embark on a journey through the world of storytelling, entertainment, and artistry.

Diverse Film Selection:

Our Movie Library boasts a diverse and extensive selection of films, ranging from timeless classics to contemporary masterpieces. Whether you're in the mood for heartwarming dramas, pulse-pounding action, side-splitting comedies, or thought-provoking documentaries, our collection has something for everyone.
